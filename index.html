ACG was built to fix that. Not by adding more consultants in suits, but by combining real construction knowledge with a smarter, faster process — so the documents that reach your principal contractor are accurate, relevant, and ready to go.

Common questions

Everything you need to know

If your question isn't answered here, just get in touch — we'll come back same day.

How does the 48-hour turnaround work?
Once you submit your enquiry with the project details, we start work immediately. You'll receive your completed documents within 48 hours — most jobs come back faster. If you have a tighter deadline, tell us upfront and we'll always confirm whether we can meet it before you commit.
Are your documents trade-specific?
Yes — every document is written for your specific trade and task, not pulled from a generic library. We cover roofing, groundworks, M&E, civils, fit-out, scaffolding, demolition, cladding, flooring and more. If your trade isn't listed, get in touch — we'll let you know if we can help.
What format do I receive the documents in?
All documents are delivered as professionally formatted Word (.docx) and PDF files — ready to submit straight to your principal contractor or client, or print and keep on site. They're branded, referenced, and formatted to the standard that gets approved.
Are you VAT registered?
No — we're not VAT registered, which means there are no hidden extras on top of our listed prices. What you see on the pricing page is exactly what you pay. Simple.
What information do you need from me?
We need the basics: your trade, the task being carried out, the site address or project name, the client or principal contractor, and your deadline. If there are specific hazards, plant, or site conditions we should know about, include those too. The more context you give us, the more accurate the documents will be.
What if my principal contractor requests changes?
We include one round of amendments on every document at no extra cost. In practice, this rarely happens — because we write documents to the standard that principal contractors working with local authorities, housing associations and NHS trusts actually require. But if revisions are needed, we'll turn them around fast.
Do you cover the whole UK?
Yes. We're based in Hull, East Yorkshire, but we serve contractors across the whole of the UK. Everything is handled remotely — you submit your details, we produce the documents, and deliver them straight to your inbox.
What's the difference between the templates and the full service?
Our templates are blank, editable documents you download and fill in yourself — perfect if you know your trade well and just need the right framework fast. The full service means we write the document for you, tailored to your specific project, trade, and client. If you're not sure which you need, the full service is the safer choice.
