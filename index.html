How it works

From enquiry to submission in three steps

No lengthy onboarding. No forms to fill in triplicate. Just tell us what you need — we handle the rest.

1

Tell us about the project

Drop us a message or fill in the quick form. We need the trade, the task, the client, and your deadline. That's it — no lengthy briefing documents, no back-and-forth before we start.

2

We get to work

Our AI-powered system produces your documentation. Every document is then reviewed and signed off by a qualified site supervisor — someone who understands the work, not just the form. Accurate, specific, and built to pass.

3

Submit with confidence

You receive completed documents within 48 hours — professionally formatted, correctly referenced, and ready to go straight to your PC or client. We don't do rewrites because we don't do rejections.
